Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is Placement Copilot really free?
Yes! The Free tier is completely free forever. You can create one resume with all basic templates at no cost. We believe everyone deserves a professional resume, regardless of their budget.
Do I need design skills to use it?
Not at all. Placement Copilot is designed for people with zero design experience. Just fill in your information, pick a template, and we'll handle the formatting and layout automatically.
Can I download my resume as a PDF?
Absolutely. Every tier includes PDF export. Pro and Premium users also get Word (.docx) and HTML export options. Your resume is yours to download and submit anywhere.
What templates are available?
We offer three professionally designed templates: Classic, Modern, and Minimal. Pro and Premium users get access to additional exclusive templates with unique layouts and color schemes.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your existing resumes and data will remain accessible on the Free tier. No long-term commitments or cancellation fees.
Is my data secure?
Your data is stored securely and encrypted at rest. We never sell or share your personal information. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your settings.
